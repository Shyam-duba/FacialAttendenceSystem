How to Add a New User:

  1. Login to the system using your administrator credentials.
  2. Navigate to the "Manage Users" section.
  3. Click on the "Add New User" button.
  4. Enter the details of the new user, including their name and student ID.
  5. Upload a clear image of the new user's face for facial recognition.
  6. Click on the "Save" or "Add User" button to add the new user to the system.

How to Use Facial Attendance:

  1. Login to the system using your administrator credentials.
  2. Navigate to the "Attendance" or "Take Attendance" section.
  3. Select the class or group for which you want to take attendance.
  4. Click on the "Start Attendance" or "Take Attendance" button.
  5. The system will start capturing images of students' faces.
  6. Once the process is complete, the attendance will be automatically marked based on facial recognition.
  7. Review and finalize the attendance records as needed.
